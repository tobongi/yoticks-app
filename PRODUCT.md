# YoTicks Product Context

## Surface

Product surface.

YoTicks is a mobile-first ticketing app for real event attendance, quick check-in, and organizer operations. The design should help people finish a task fast, even when they are tired, in a queue, on poor devices, or not comfortable reading long text.

## Primary Users

- Attendees in fast-moving city environments who want to discover events, reserve tickets, and open their QR code quickly.
- Door staff and organizers who need to scan, verify, and manage entry with minimal friction.
- Users with low literacy or low patience who rely more on icons, counts, color, and clear affordances than on dense copy.

## Product Priorities

- Fast comprehension in under 3 seconds.
- Main action visible in the first viewport.
- Count-first UI for ticketing and scan operations.
- Minimal reading load.
- Friendly but not childish.
- Warm, local, energetic, lived-in.

## Brand Voice

- warm
- direct
- street-smart

## Visual References

- transport signage
- festival wristbands
- point-of-sale terminals
- venue wayfinding systems

## Anti-References

- sterile SaaS dashboards
- luxury fintech minimalism
- over-explained onboarding
- neon gamer interfaces

## Interaction Rules

- One obvious next action per screen.
- Large touch targets.
- Color never carries meaning alone; pair it with text, icons, or counts.
- Short labels beat explanatory paragraphs.
- Status should appear as numbers, chips, and progress before prose.
